Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe or upgrade my plan?

Subscriptions are managed on our website at usmle.learnatopia.com. Sign in and navigate to the Pricing section to choose or upgrade your plan.

Can I access the app on multiple devices?

Yes. Your account can be used on any device. Simply log in with your registered email and your progress will sync automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime via the Pricing page (on web) or by visiting usmle.learnatopia.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

What happens to my data if I delete my account?

All your data — including progress, bookmarks, and flashcards — will be permanently deleted within 7 days of your request and cannot be recovered.

Are the questions written by medical professionals?

Our question bank is developed and reviewed with medical education standards in mind. AI-generated questions are clearly labeled and reviewed for accuracy.

How do I reset my progress?

You can clear individual session history from the Analytics page. For a full reset, contact our support team.
